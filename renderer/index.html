Welcome.

A free teleprompter for pitches, podcasts, YouTube, auditions, and interviews - anyone who speaks on camera or on stage.

This is a standalone app on your machine. Use it on a second screen, offline, or anywhere you speak. Your scripts stay local.

  • Scroll any script on screen - content, sides, talking points
  • Speed, font size, mirror, fullscreen
  • AI formatting is optional - only if you add your own key

Optional: AI formatting

Non-essential. Without a key, paste a script already broken into short lines (or use Format (local)).

If you add a key, it is stored only on this device (encrypted when the OS allows). It is sent only to Anthropic when you click AI Format - never to RiseDrivers.

Get a key at console.anthropic.com - you pay Anthropic directly.
